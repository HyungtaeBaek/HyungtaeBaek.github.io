In this paper, we introduce the notion of a $w$-Hilbert domain and investigate its basic properties.
More precisely, we explore its relationship with
Hilbert domains, strong Mori domains, and UMT domains
by providing various examples using $D+M$ constructions.
Furthermore, we establish necessary and sufficient conditions for
the polynomial ring and the Anderson ring to be $w$-Hilbert domains,
and compare the $w$-dimension of the polynomial ring with that of its base ring.
